## What It Is

A personal MTG deck builder and simulator. Private tool for deck construction, card browsing, and (eventually) playtesting. Accessible via Vercel link to anyone with the URL.

## What It Is Not

Not a public SaaS. Not a replacement for Moxfield/Archidekt for competitive deck sharing. Not a rules engine simulator for tournament-grade play testing.
